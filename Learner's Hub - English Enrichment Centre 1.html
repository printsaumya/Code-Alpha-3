FOUNDER'S NOTE

My journey as a teacher began with 3 students in the year 2004. In the last eighteen years, my team and I have educated more than 5,000 students as of 2021.

As I am a passionate educator, I had an opportunity to grow and be a lifelong learner. This pandemic gave me a robust unlearning and relearning experience with new Online technology. New systems, new skills and restructuring of our content and curriculum has taken Learner's Hub to the next level of excellence.

Learner's Hub mission is to make learning fun, engaging and interactive for our students i.e. to Educate-Engage-Empower. Our aim is to create an interest in English Language and empower our students by inculcating reading, writing, comprehending skills and speaking skills. We intend to shape independent thinkers and communicators to express language confidently and effectively.

My team and I ensure that children excel in our Online and Physical classes. The students learn better skills through nurturing and compassion by teaching with their specific learning styles of auditory - kinesthetic - visual.




Nisha Sanghvi
